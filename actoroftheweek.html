Actor of the month: Mitchell Patterson

Mitchell has earned Actor of the Month due to his overwhelming enthusiasm and willingness to participate in all theatre programs offered to him. Whether it's a musical, comedy, or he's just in the background, you can count on him to be there and work on making the play great. His tenacity and blunt attitude means that he's going to give the truth. Thank you for all your hard work Mitchell.
